Design System

A GitBook-inspired knowledge site for Granular

Core pattern

Documentation as product strategy

Each document becomes a navigable knowledge module, with consistent structure and a sticky table of contents for fast scanning.

Palette

Warm paper, ink, and trust green

Off-white surfaces, dark ink text, restrained green, and amber accents support a financial product that feels clear and private.
